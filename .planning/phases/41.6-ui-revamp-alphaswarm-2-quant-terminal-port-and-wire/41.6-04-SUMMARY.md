**3. [Rule 3 — Blocking] BracketDeepDive case-mismatch on bracket key**

The bracket descriptor is normalized at render time, not at the data layer (caller boundary). BRACKET_COLORS lookup still uses the lowercase key, so the takeover header pill renders with the correct color.
- **Files modified:** `frontend/src/components/app_v2.tsx`, `frontend/src/components/bracket_deep.tsx`
