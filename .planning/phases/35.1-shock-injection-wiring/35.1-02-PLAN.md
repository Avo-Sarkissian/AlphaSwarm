Thread a `consume_shock: Callable[[], str | None] | None = None` parameter through `run_simulation`; before Round 2 AND Round 3 dispatch, call `consume_shock()`, and if it returns non-None, augment that round's `user_message` with a `[MARKET SHOCK]` marker and call `graph_manager.write_shock_event(cycle_id, shock_text, injected_before_round=<round>)`. This completes the fix documented in BUGFIX-CONTEXT.md B1 and unblocks Phase 36 (Report Viewer) from surfacing empty shock data.
