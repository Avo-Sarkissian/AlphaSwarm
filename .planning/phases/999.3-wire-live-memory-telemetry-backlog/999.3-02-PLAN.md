    4. **FAIL (5a):** Footer shows a real RAM% during IDLE.
    5. Start a simulation (any seed). After ROUND_1 begins, the footer must transition to showing real values.
    6. **PASS (5b):** Footer shows `RAM: 67%   |   TPS: 14.2   |   Queue: 3   |   Slots: 8` (or similar real values) during simulation.

    **Step 6 — Frontend dev console clean**

    2. Open dashboard, let it sit IDLE for 30 seconds.
    3. **PASS:** No new errors or warnings about `governor_metrics`, `memory_percent`, or `undefined`.
