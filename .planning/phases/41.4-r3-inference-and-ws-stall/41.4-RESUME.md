```
fix(41.4-02): Bug B — swap to qwen3 family for parallel-capable worker

Root cause: ollama 0.21.2 hardcodes Parallel=1 for qwen35/qwen35moe
architecture (issues #14510, #14621, #14879 — open, no upstream fix).
The 14-20 min/call R3 latency reported in 41.4-CONTEXT.md was queue-
time accumulation on the 100th serially-stacked worker call, not per-
inference latency (per-inference is ~5s on this hardware).

Worker: qwen3.5:9b → qwen3:8b (qwen3moe arch, parallel proven on M1 Max).
Orchestrator: qwen3.5:35b → qwen3.6:27b-q4_K_M (smaller + newer; reasoning
default-on but call sites pass think=False — empirical probe showed
think=True adds ~265s/call for marginal quality gain on consensus
aggregation; see decision log for revisit triggers).

Empirical: 8 concurrent worker calls now finish in 7.5s wall (vs 45s
serial-stacked before), 100% GPU placement, ~14 tok/s/slot.
NUM_PARALLEL lowered 16 → 4 — N=16 caused 103 GB KV allocation that
spilled the model to CPU and collapsed throughput.

Estimated full sim: ~6 min (vs ~26 min on prior stack, ~5x speedup).
Full rationale, options NOT chosen, and revisit guide in
.planning/phases/41.4-r3-inference-and-ws-stall/41.4-MODEL-DECISION-LOG.md.
```
